Vanessa Laird

Bio

Vanessa Laird is a Licensed Psychologist is Saratoga Springs, New York. She focuses in Psychological Assessment and Psychotherapy. She is a member of the American Psychological Association and frequently collaborates with researchers and scientists in to study the scientist-practitioner model in counseling psychology programs.

Education

Texas A&M University | College Station, TX

  • Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology | 2011 - 2017
  • M.Ed. in Counseling Psychology | 2011 - 2012

University of Texas at Austin | Austin, TX

  • B.A. in Psychology | 2006 - 2011

Experience

  • Saratoga Hospital | Psychologist | December 2019 - Present
  • Albany Medical Center | Psychologist | October 2018 - October 2019
  • Albany Medical Center | Postdoctoral Fellow | September 2017 - September 2018
  • Albany Psychology Internship Consortium | Clinical Psychology Intern | September 2016 - August 2017
